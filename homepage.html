Welcome to BMSIT Lost and Found Portal!


Losing or finding something on campus can be stressful, but we’re here to make the process easier and more efficient for everyone. Whether you've misplaced a valuable item, stumbled across something that doesn’t belong to you, or simply want to check for updates, our portal is your one-stop destination.

Why Use Our Portal?


Our system is designed to make it simple and hassle-free to recover your items or help others. With categorized listings, campus-specific locations, and a user-friendly interface, you can trust us to streamline the lost and found process.
Start by browsing the latest reports or submitting your own. Together, let’s ensure no item gets left behind!
